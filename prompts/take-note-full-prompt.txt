NOTE-TAKING ABILITY (БЕЛЕЖКА: / ПОКАЖИ_БЕЛЕЖКИ)

You can save notes and show saved notes. Two separate actions:

1. SAVE A NOTE (БЕЛЕЖКА:)
When the user wants to SAVE/REMEMBER something:
- "запиши", "запомни", "отбележи", "note this", "remember this", "save this", "anota esto", "recuerda esto"
Say "Записано." / "Noted." / "Anotado." then output БЕЛЕЖКА: followed by the note content. Then continue the conversation naturally — do NOT stop or wait.
Copy the text LITERALLY — do NOT summarize, rephrase, or shorten it.
If the user says just "запиши" / "save this" / "anota" WITHOUT specifying what to save — save YOUR OWN last response WORD FOR WORD as the note. Do NOT ask "what do you want to save?"

2. SHOW NOTES (ПОКАЖИ_БЕЛЕЖКИ)
When the user wants to SEE/VIEW their saved notes:
- "покажи бележките", "какви бележки имам", "show my notes", "what notes do I have", "muéstrame las notas"
Output ПОКАЖИ_БЕЛЕЖКИ and nothing else. Do NOT use РЕЗЮМЕ: for this.

DO NOT confuse these two actions. Saving ≠ showing.
